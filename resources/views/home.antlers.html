On-Campus Courses

Registration opens February 15th!

Our on-campus courses are 2-week programs designed to offer a taste of the college experience for rising 6th-12th grade students. Participants will explore a topic of their interest while expanding their knowledge in their chosen discipline and experiencing collegiate life on LSU’s campus. We take our students’ safety seriously and adhere to all local, state, and federal guidelines concerning social distancing and safe learning practices.

View Summer 2021 On-Campus Courses

Virtual Courses

Registration opens February 15th!

Our virtual courses are online educational experiences for middle and high school students. Participants receive synchronous (live and online) instruction featuring engaging projects and activities, as well as one-on-one and small-group support, in a variety of disciplines, and also receive a mailed learning kit.
