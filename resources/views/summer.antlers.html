Virtual and On-Campus Summer Programs at LSU

LSU Pre-College offers top-notch educational experiences for 6th-12th grade students each summer. From biology to math to engineering, there’s something for every student in our lineup of virtual and on-campus courses.

+ More About Our Learning Approach

Learning Virtually: With our virtual courses, you'll receive synchronous (live and online) instruction featuring engaging projects and activities, as well as one-on-one and small-group support, in a variety of disciplines. Learning virtually can be challenging - but it doesn’t have to be. With student engagement and learning as our primary goals, our courses incorporate live virtual class every weekday, hands-on activities and projects, and a mailed learning kit with course supplies for every course. It's virtual learning at its best!

Learning On-Campus: We know everyone is as excited as we are to get back to campus! Participants in our on-campus courses will explore a topic of their interest while experiencing collegiate life on LSU’s campus. We take our students’ safety seriously and adhere to all local, state, and federal guidelines concerning social distancing and safe learning practices.
