LSU Pre-College: On-Campus Courses

Applications open February 15th!

LSU Pre-College On-Campus Courses are 2-week programs designed to offer a taste of the college experience for rising 9th-12th grade students. Participants will explore a topic of their interest while expanding their knowledge in their chosen discipline and experiencing collegiate life on LSU’s campus. We take our students’ safety seriously and adhere to all local, state, and federal guidelines concerning social distancing and safe learning practices.
