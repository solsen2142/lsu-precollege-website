LSU Summer Experiences are 1- to 2-week on-campus experiences for rising 7th-12th grade students put on by various LSU departments and supported by grants and sponsors. Less rigorous than the Pre-College Summer Academy, these engaging and educational camps give you a head start in your STEM education and enrich your summer with engaging and educational activities! Our 2020 summer experiences are shown below:

College of Engineering

REHAMS

The LSU College of Engineering is proud to offer the REHAMS residential engineering summer camp to qualified high school students. This program gives high school students the chance to explore, create, experiment, build, code, design, and compete in a variety of engineering and college preparatory activities. The camp is held on LSU’s Baton Rouge campus where college faculty and college students interact with attendees through a number of hands-on workshops and activities. The signature event is the week-long “Design Project Competition” in which students compete in teams to design, build, and present a project.

Dates: June 7th - 13th, 2020
Type: Residential
Grades: Current 10th - 11th
GPA Required: 3.0 or greater
Tuition: $600 Early Bird / $650 Regular
Scholarships: A limited number of need-based scholarships are available.
Contact: rehams@eng.lsu.edu
