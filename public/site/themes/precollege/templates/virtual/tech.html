All students in the LSU Pre-College Virtual Academy will need to meet the following technology requirements in order to participate in any of the courses offered. Certain courses may have additional requirements - check the course descriptions here for more info.

Student tech requirements:

  • Access to an email for essential communication from LSU Pre-College
  • Reliable speakers or headphones to hear instructors and participate in class discussions
  • Webcam with working camera and microphone to participate in activities and projects during class
  • Computer with 1GB of available RAM and 2GHz or faster processor running one of the following operating systems:
    • Windows 7 or newer
    • Mac OSX 10.10 or newer
    • ChromeOS (not recommended)
  • Access to reliable internet with at least 2k kbps available bandwidth
  • Up-to date Chrome or Firefox browser with cookies enabled
  • Adobe Acrobat Reader and Adobe Flash Player (version 11.2 or higher)
  • Reliable access to Google and the Google domain
  • Access to a word processor such as Microsoft Word or Google Docs
