Coronavirus (COVID-19) Information

LSU Pre-College is carefully monitoring developments related to the Novel Coronavirus, also known as COVID-19. We will keep this page updated as new information arises about its potential impacts on LSU and on our summer programs.

Current Program Status

No cancellations

Currently, all programs are scheduled to take place this summer as planned. We are closely following the issue and working with university administration to ensure the safety and well-being of our participants and the university as a whole.

The most updated information regarding the impact of coronavirus on LSU can be found at www.lsu.edu/coronavirus.
