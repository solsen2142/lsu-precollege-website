Summer Academy Tuition

Below are the tuition rates for the 2020 LSU Pre-College Summer Academy. Additionally, a late fee of $250 will be applied to any payments received after May 15th, 2020.

Early Bird Deadline: February 29th, 2020

Regular Application Deadline: April 30th, 2020

Tuition Deposit: To receive the Early Bird discount, we must receive your $1000 tuition deposit by February 29th. The application fee is counted towards your tuition deposit balance.

Payment Deadline: Full tuition payment must be made by May 15th, 2020.

Scholarships & Finanicial Aid: We are committed to making the LSU Pre-College Summer Academy affordable for all students and their families. A limited number of scholarships and financial aid awards are available based on the needs of each family. Click here for more information on Scholarships & Financial Aid.

Residential Tuition

Early-Bird: $3950
Regular: $4250

Commuting Tuition

Early-Bird: $2750
Regular: $2950

Tuition Includes:

Tuition for commuting students includes 8 1/2 hours of supervision and instruction each day and lunch every weekday of the program. Tuition for residential students includes 8 1/2 hours of instruction each day, 24/7 supervision, and housing, breakfast, lunch, and dinner for every day of the program.

Summer Academy tuition is nonrefundable. Refunds are offered at the discretion of LSU and are subject to an administrative fee.

Financial Aid and Scholarships

We will offer a limited number of full and partial scholarships/financial aid this summer. If you would like to be considered for an LSU Pre-College Summer Academy scholarship, please indicate this when submitting your application.
