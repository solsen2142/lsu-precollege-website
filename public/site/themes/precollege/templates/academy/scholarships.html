Scholarships & Financial Aid Application

We are committed to making the LSU Pre-College Summer Academy affordable for all students and their families. A limited number of scholarships and financial aid awards are available based on the needs of each family.

To be considered for full or partial scholarships, please complete the Scholarships & Financial Aid Application below after applying for the LSU Pre-College Summer Academy. The application can be found at this link, and application fees are waived for financial aid applicants.

Deadline: Full scholarship decisions will be made by March 30th, 2020. Partial scholarships and financial aid awards will be made on a rolling basis until all funds are allocated.
