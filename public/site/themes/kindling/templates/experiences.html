LSU Summer Experiences are 1- to 2-week on-campus experiences for rising 7th-12th grade students. Less rigorous than the Pre-College Summer Academy, these engaging and educational camps give you a head start in your STEM education and help prepare you for an exciting career in multiple fields. Our 2020 summer experiences are shown below:
